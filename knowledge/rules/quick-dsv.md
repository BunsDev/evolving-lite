# Quick DSV (Decompose - Suspend - Validate)

Before acting on complex, open-ended, or exploratory requests, run 3 questions (~30 seconds):

1. **Decompose:** What are the 2-3 key claims in this request?
2. **Suspend:** What alternative interpretation haven't I considered?
3. **Validate:** Which claim am I least sure about? (Validate that first)

Skip for trivial/explicit tasks (single file, known location, clear command).
If question 2 reveals a plausible alternative: raise it before proceeding.
